What we do

We associate with company/supplier based on few eligibility criteria such as turnover, work strength, working model etc. Once the supplier is associated with us, we provide end to end support such as gathering projects based on the suppliers expertise, recording interest to BID for the project, supporting in sending resources to UK and more. WestGate IT HUB will assist in end to end process from bidding the project to getting the amount in account.
